# Directive: "The Discovery Session" - Conversational Client Onboarding

## Mission Statement
Transform the boring multi-step form into a **conversational discovery experience** that feels like chatting with a friendly expert. The goal is to extract everything needed to build a stunning website while making the client feel excited about their project - not overwhelmed by forms.

---

## Core Philosophy

### The Problem with Traditional Onboarding
- Forms feel like homework
- Non-technical clients don't know what "brand hex codes" means
- Dropouts happen when people feel confused or overwhelmed
- Generic questions get generic answers (useless for design)

### The SEOJack Approach
- **Conversational, not transactional** - feels like talking to a designer friend
- **Smart defaults** - pre-fill based on industry, let them customize
- **Show, don't ask** - use visual choices instead of text inputs where possible
- **Progressive disclosure** - only ask what's relevant based on previous answers
- **Celebrate progress** - make completing each section feel rewarding

---

## Technical Implementation Notes

### State Management
- Save progress after EVERY question (not just each phase)
- Use localStorage for anonymous users, sync to Firebase on account creation
- Allow "Save & Continue Later" with magic link sent to email

### Smart Defaults by Industry
Create JSON config files for each industry containing:
```javascript
{
  industry: "plumber",
  defaultServices: ["Boiler Repair", "Emergency Callouts", "Bathroom Installation"],
  commonCertifications: ["Gas Safe", "CIPHE"],
  typicalCustomers: ["Homeowners"],
  suggestedVibe: "Friendly & Approachable",
  colorSuggestion: "Ocean",
  sampleQuestions: ["24-hour emergency service?", "Free quotes?"],
  competitorExamples: ["pimlicoplumbers.com", "dyno.com"]
}
```

### Progress Indication
- Use a progress bar that fills smoothly (not step jumps)
- Show encouraging messages: "You're 60% done - most people finish in 4 minutes"
- Display a "Your website is taking shape!" preview that updates with their choices

### The Live Preview (Differentiator Feature)
As they answer questions, show a LIVE PREVIEW of their website being "built":
- Phase 1 complete â†’ Show header with their business name
- Phase 2 complete â†’ Show services section populated
- Phase 4 complete â†’ Apply their chosen color scheme
- This creates dopamine hits and reduces abandonment

### Escape Hatches
- Every screen has "I'm not sure / Skip for now" option
- Chat widget always visible: "Stuck? Ask us anything"
- Phone number visible: "Prefer to talk? Call us"

### Mobile-First Design
- Assume 70%+ will do this on their phone
- Big tap targets, minimal typing
- Voice input option for free text fields
- Photo upload directly from camera
